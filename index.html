Tokyo

Summer is the warm season. Days are longer, the air is hotter, and people often go hiking, swimming, or have picnics. Summer is a time for relaxation and freedom.

Frankfurt

Autumn is the vibrant season of deciduous trees. Leaves turn yellow, orange, and red, then fall to the ground. Autumn days are cool but still pleasant.

London

Winter is cold and snowy. Trees are bare, nature is quieter, and people bundle up in warm clothes. Winter is associated with Christmas and New Year’s celebrations.

New York

Spring is the time of renewal and blossoming. After the cold winter months, plants emerge from the ground, trees sprout leaves, and birds return. Spring is colorful and fresh.
